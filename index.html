Piazza Panic

This website details our developement of a single-player game that requires managing the staff around a kitchen, who will be preparing various dishes requested by customers coming into the Piazza Restaurant.

Stakeholders

One of our customers, Konstantinos Barmpis, is interested in trying to market and sell our game. We have had constant contact with this customer throughout this project.When we had design and requirement decisions we had to convince him of the validity of these assumptions and decisions. Our other customer is the University of York Communications Office. They are wishing to use this game for promotional activites. We communicate with this customer through Konstantinos.
